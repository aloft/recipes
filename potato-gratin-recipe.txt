Recipe for potato gratin
6 potatoes
1/2 cup cream
1/2 cup grated Gruyere
Preheat oven to 375.  Grease baking dish with butter and garlic.  Slice potatoes thinly.  Spread 1/2 of the potatoes in baking dish.  Top with half of the grated Gruyere, then drizzle half of the cream.  Sprinkle with salt.  Layer rest of the potatoes on top.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and remaining cream.  Sprinkle with sale.  Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is lightly browned.
